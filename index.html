Center for Statistics and Computer Sciences

The Center serves as:

  • Place for statistical consultation in the formulation statistical plans and study design.
  • Place for data management analysis.
  • Venue for seminars, workshops, conferences, symposiums and tutorials to target clients.
  • Hub for all researchers in the field of mathematics, statistics, and computing sciences.

Center for Statistics and Computing Sciences

Center's Vision

The center envisions itself as central place for statistical solutions and research in the field of statistics and computing sciences in the Region.

Center's Goals
  1. Enriched students’ skills in statistical processes.
  2. Improved statistical practices of student and faculty.
  3. Reasonable statistical services.
  4. Quality and honest statistical outputs.
  5. Enhanced capabilities of statisticians.
  6. Regulated and centralized statistical fees.
  7. Recognized research output in the field of statistics and computing sciences
  8. Prompt delivery of statistical outputs.
Organizational Structure

The Center for Statistics and Computing Sciences is under the Vice President for Research, Extension & Innovation. It is manned by a University Center for Statistics and Computing Sciences Head who will monitor the overall operations of the different component campuses including the School of Advanced Studies and Open University System. The University Head is assisted by the Campus Heads, Statisticians, and Encoders.

The main office of the Center for Statistics and Computing Sciences is situated in Lingayen campus with satellite centers located in all the campuses: Asingan, Binmaley, Sta. Maria, San Carlos, Infanta, Urdaneta, Alaminos, and Bayambang.

Rationale

The Center for Statistics and Computing Sciences serves as central place for all statistical solutions and mathematical researches in the University. Specifically, it will provide statistical consultation that includes study design (e.g. sample size calculation, statistical design, etc.), data management and analysis, statistical reporting as well as short courses and seminars. The Center is adequately staffed and is capable for serving the consulting needs in the University. It will also tailor to the particular challenges confronting the needs of the students and other target clienteles.

Coverage of the Unit

The center was established to improve the conduct of the researchers of the students, faculty and other researchers of the University and will be under the Vice President for research and Extension. It will also cater clientless outside of the university.

Services we offer:

1. Initial Consultation

This service includes checking of research problem, validation of data gathering instrument, and suggestion of statistical design, sampling procedure, and statistical tool.

2. Data Encoding and Cleaning

3. Data Analysis and Interpretation of Results

4. Output Checking

5. Conduct Seminar / Training/ Tutorials
